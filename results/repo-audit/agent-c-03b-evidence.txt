== Duplicate Pair Analysis ==

### Hero.tsx vs HomepageHero.tsx
- Hero.tsx: GENERIC reusable hero component with configurable props (title, subtitle, variant, backgroundImage, videoBackground, showButton, etc.)
  - Used in 20+ routes: about, projects, gallery, planning, solutions/*, showrooms, portfolio, privacy, terms, downloads, service, tracking, social, trusted-by, news, imprint, refund-and-return-policy, sustainability, templates, career, contact, support
  - Supports 3 variants: default, small, cinema
  - Parallax scroll effect with framer-motion
- HomepageHero.tsx: HOMEPAGE-SPECIFIC hero with hardcoded content from data/site/homepage
  - Used ONLY in app/(site)/page.tsx (the homepage)
  - Features: rotating image carousel, word-level reveal animations, glass proof panel, progress dots
  - Imports HOMEPAGE_HERO_CONTENT and HOMEPAGE_HERO_IMAGES
- CONCLUSION: NOT duplicates. Different responsibilities. Hero is generic/reusable, HomepageHero is homepage-specific.

### TrustStrip.tsx vs HomeTrustStrip.tsx
- TrustStrip.tsx: Renders KPI stats (yearsExperience, projectsDelivered, clientOrganisations, locationsServed)
  - Takes BusinessStats as prop
  - Used in multiple routes for displaying business metrics
- HomeTrustStrip.tsx: Renders logo marquee/carousel of client logos
  - Uses HOMEPAGE_TRUST_CONTENT from data/site/homepage
  - Used ONLY in app/(site)/page.tsx (the homepage)
- CONCLUSION: NOT duplicates. Different responsibilities. TrustStrip shows KPI numbers, HomeTrustStrip shows client logo marquee.
